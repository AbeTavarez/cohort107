JavaScript Mathematical Operators

    An mathematical operator is a symbol that produces a result based on two values or variables.

  • Addition (+): Add two numbers together or combine two strings.
  • Subtraction (-): performs a subtraction from two values.
  • Multiplication (*): performs a multiplication from two values.
  • Division (/): performs a division from two values.
  • Modulus or Remainder (%): returns the remainder of a division.
  • Exponentiation (**): raises a number to the power of the other.
  • Increment (++): increments a value by 1.
  • Decrement (--): decrements a value by 1.
